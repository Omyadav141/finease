ICICI Bank Current Account

Designed for businesses, professionals, and entrepreneurs, the ICICI Bank Current Account provides essential banking services with a range of features tailored to various business needs.

Open Current Account Instantly Online

Types of Current Accounts

Type Description
Basic Current Account For small businesses with moderate transaction volumes.
Premium Current Account For high transaction volumes with value-added services.

Features

Feature Description
Overdraft Facility Pre-approved overdraft limits based on business performance.
Cash Deposit & Withdrawal Free cash deposits and withdrawals up to a specified limit.
Dedicated Relationship Manager Assigned manager for handling business transactions and providing personalized service.
Multiple Branch Access Access to any ICICI branch for business banking across the country.

Eligibility Criteria

Category
Businesses, Professionals, Entrepreneurs
Available to businesses, professionals, and entrepreneurs. Specific eligibility criteria may vary based on the account type.

Charges

Charges are based on transaction volumes and the specific services availed. For detailed fee structure, please refer to the [ICICI Bank Current Account Charges Page](https://www.icicibank.com/business-banking/current-account.page).

Documents Required

Document Type
Business Proof
Proof of business registration, partnership deed, or certificate of incorporation.
Identity Proof
Aadhaar, PAN card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.
Address Proof
Utility bill, Aadhaar, Passport, rental agreement, etc.
Photographs: Passport-sized photographs of the account holders and authorized signatories.

Account Opening Process

Accounts can be opened online through the ICICI Bank portal or offline at a branch. For online applications, upload the required documents and complete the KYC process. Offline applications may take 2-5 business days for verification and account activation.

Additional Information

For more details and to apply for the Current Account, visit the ICICI Bank [Current Account Page](https://www.icicibank.com/business-banking/current-account.page).
