Bank of India (BOI) Gold Loan

1. Loan Amount

  • Up to 75% to 90% of the gold’s market value (based on purity and weight).
  • Loan amount ranges from a few thousand rupees to ₹25 lakh.

2. Interest Rates

  • 10% to 14% p.a., depending on the loan amount and applicant profile.

3. Repayment Tenure

  • Tenure ranges from 6 months to 36 months.

4. Features

  • Quick Disbursal: Loan approval and disbursal can happen on the same day.
  • Minimal Documentation: Only basic identity and gold-related documents required.
  • No Credit Score Check: Since the loan is secured by gold, no credit score check is needed.
  • Flexible Repayment Options: EMI, bullet repayment, or partial repayment options available.
  • Prepayment: Allowed with no penalty after a specified period.
  • Insurance: Gold is insured for the loan tenure.

5. Eligibility

  • Indian residents.
  • Minimum age of 18 years; maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years.
  • Must possess gold ornaments or coins of at least 22K purity.
  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar card, PAN card, etc.).
  • No minimum income requirement as the loan is secured by gold.

6. Documents Required

  • Gold ornaments or coins.
  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar, Passport, PAN card, etc.).
  • Proof of address (electricity bill, passport, etc.).
