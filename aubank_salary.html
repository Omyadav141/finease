Features and Benefits

Features

  • High-interest rates: Earn up to 7.25%* p.a. with monthly interest payouts. Click here for details.
  • State-of-the-art Digital Banking: 24x7 access to funds through AU 0101 App or AU 0101 NetBanking. Book flights, hotels, recharge your mobile, and more.
  • Easily accessible: Our digital-first approach and extensive network of touchpoints make banking simple and convenient.
  • Dedicated Relationship Officer: Your single point of contact for all banking and financial needs.

Benefits

  • Zero Balance Salary Account: No minimum balance requirement.
  • Instant Salary Account Opening: Open your account instantly through TAB Banking.
  • Banking at your convenience: Perform transactions and service requests from home using Video Banking.
  • Paperless banking: Deposit funds without the need for a deposit slip.
  • Wide range of products: Access Term Deposits, Credit Cards, Lockers, Mutual Funds, Insurance, and more.
  • Asset Offering: Special rates on Retail Asset products for salaried customers.

Salary Account Benefits for Employers

  • Comprehensive banking solutions with a Current Account for business needs.
  • Simplified Payroll Solution and online salary upload facility.
  • Instant intimation and salary credit to employee accounts.
  • Dedicated Relationship Manager and extended banking hours.

Eligibility Criteria

You can open a Salary Savings Account if you are an on-roll employee of a company empanelled for the salary relationship with AU Small Finance Bank.

How to Apply

  • Open Salary Account Online: Fill out the online form, and a representative will contact you.
  • Contact your Relationship Manager: Request assistance from your HR team to get the contact details of AU Small Finance Bank Relationship Manager.
  • Visit your nearest branch: Walk in with your KYC documents and photographs to start the process.

Documents Required

Prepare the following for online or branch account opening:

  • Aadhaar Card Number
  • Original PAN Card
  • Employment Confirmation proof (OTP verification, offer letter, salary slip, or Employee ID card)
  • For AU Royale Salary Account: Application form, recent photograph, and KYC documents.
