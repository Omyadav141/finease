SBI - Student Account

SBI offers two types of Savings Accounts for minors: Pehla Kadam and Pehli Udaan. These accounts are designed to teach children the importance of saving money while providing them with modern banking features.

Features

Feature Details
Monthly Average Balance (MAB) Requirement not applicable
Maximum Balance Rs. 10 lakh
Cheque Book Pehla Kadam: Available with specially designed personalized chequebook (10 leaves).
Pehli Udaan: Available if the minor can sign uniformly, with specially designed personalized chequebook (10 leaves).
Photo ATM-cum-Debit Card Pehla Kadam: Child's photo embossed card with withdrawal/POS limit of Rs. 5,000.
Pehli Udaan: Photo embossed card with withdrawal/POS limit of Rs. 5,000.
Mobile Banking Pehla Kadam: Viewing rights with limited transactions (Bill payment, Top ups). Per day limit Rs. 2,000.
Pehli Udaan: Viewing rights with limited transactions (Bill payment, Top ups, IMPS). Per day limit Rs. 2,000.
Auto Sweep Facility Minimum threshold of Rs. 20,000. Sweep in multiples of Rs. 1,000 with a minimum of Rs. 10,000.

Eligibility

Account Type Eligibility
Pehla Kadam Minor of any age, jointly opened with parent/guardian.
Pehli Udaan Minors above the age of 10 who can sign uniformly. Opened in the sole name of the minor.

KYC Requirements

Account Type KYC Requirements
Pehla Kadam Date of Birth proof of the minor + KYC of the Parent.
Pehli Udaan Date of Birth proof of the minor + KYC of the Parent.

Other Features

Feature Details

3.00% p.a. (Rs.10 Cr. and above)
Transferability Accounts can be transferred to any SBI branch without changing the account number.
Nomination Facility Available and recommended.
