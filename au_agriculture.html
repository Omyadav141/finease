AU Small Finance Bank Agriculture Loan

1. Types of Agriculture Loans

  • Crop Loans: Short-term loans for purchasing seeds, fertilizers, and other inputs for crop cultivation.
  • Agri Term Loans: Financing for longer-term agricultural projects like farm equipment or storage construction.
  • Tractor Loans: Loans specifically for purchasing tractors and other farming machinery.
  • Dairy Loans: Funding for dairy farming activities, including animal purchase and infrastructure development.
  • Farm Mechanization Loans: Loans for purchasing irrigation systems or advanced farming equipment.
  • Loans for Horticulture & Allied Activities: Financing for activities like fruit farming, poultry, fisheries, and more.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Borrowers should typically be between 18-70 years old.
  • Occupation: Applicants should be farmers, self-employed in agriculture, or rural entrepreneurs.
  • Credit History: A reasonable credit score may be required, though AU Small Finance Bank may offer flexible terms for rural applicants.

3. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, Voter ID, or PAN Card.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, ration card, or any valid government-issued address proof.
  • Land Ownership Proof: Documents proving ownership or lease of agricultural land.
  • Income Proof: Agricultural income certificate, bank statements, or tax receipts.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Loan-Specific Documents: Quotations for machinery, bills for equipment, or relevant invoices.

4. Loan Amount & Tenure

  • Loan Amount: Typically ranges from INR 10,000 up to a few lakh rupees, depending on loan type.
  • Repayment Tenure: Crop loans usually have a tenure of up to 1 year, while term loans may extend to 5-7 years.

5. Interest Rates & Fees

  • Interest Rate: Typically between 9-15% p.a., depending on loan type and applicant profile.
  • Processing Fee: Generally between 0.5-2% of the loan amount.
  • Other Fees: Documentation charges, late payment penalties, and foreclosure charges may apply.

6. Features & Benefits

  • Flexible Repayment Options: Repayment aligned with harvest cycles for crop loans.
  • Minimal Documentation: Simplified process for rural customers.
  • Quick Disbursal: Fast processing to ensure timely funding for agricultural needs.
  • Customized Solutions: Loan products tailored to meet specific agricultural and rural client needs.
  • Insurance Coverage: Many agricultural loans offer insurance to protect crops and assets.

7. How to Apply

  • Branch Visit: Apply by visiting the nearest AU Small Finance Bank branch.
  • Online Application: Some agri-loans may be applied through the official website or mobile app.
  • Customer Support: Contact customer support for inquiries related to agricultural loans via helpline.

8. Loan Approval Process

  • Upon application submission, AU Small Finance Bank assesses the applicant’s creditworthiness, land ownership, and repayment capacity.
  • Upon approval, funds are disbursed directly to the applicant’s account or as required for equipment purchases.
