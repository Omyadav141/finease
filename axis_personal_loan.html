Axis Bank Personal Loan

1. Eligibility Criteria

Salaried Individuals:

  • Age: 21 to 60 years.
  • Employment: Minimum of 6 months with the current employer and a total work experience of at least 1 year.
  • Minimum Monthly Income: Typically starts at ₹15,000, though this can vary depending on the city and loan amount.

Self-Employed Individuals:

  • Age: 24 to 65 years.
  • Business Continuity: Minimum of 3 years in the current business.
  • Income: Proof of stable income over the past few years is required.

2. Loan Amount and Tenure

  • Loan Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh.
  • Loan Tenure: Ranges from 12 to 60 months, allowing flexibility based on the borrower’s ability to repay.

3. Interest Rates

  • Rates: Typically range from 10.49% to 20% per annum, depending on the borrower’s profile, credit score, and income stability.
  • Processing Fees: Generally around 1.5% to 2% of the loan amount (plus GST).
  • Prepayment Charges: Up to 5% of the outstanding loan amount if the loan is closed within the lock-in period of one year.

4. Key Features

  • Quick Processing: Loan approval and disbursal are often processed quickly, especially for existing Axis Bank customers.
  • Flexible Repayment Options: Tenures up to 60 months allow for flexibility in planning repayment.
  • No Collateral Required: Unsecured loan, so no need to pledge any assets.
  • Part-Payment Options: Allowed after 12 EMIs, which can reduce the principal and interest payable.

5. Documentation Required

  • Identity Proof: PAN Card, Passport, Voter ID, Aadhaar Card.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, rental agreement, or Aadhaar.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Latest salary slips (3 months), bank statements (3 months), Form 16.
    • Self-Employed: Income Tax Returns (ITR), audited financials, business continuity proof.
  • Photograph: Passport-sized recent photo.

6. Application Process

  • Online: Applicants can apply directly on Axis Bank’s website or mobile app. The application involves filling in personal details, uploading documents, and providing employment and income details.
  • Branch: Visit any Axis Bank branch with required documents and consult with a bank representative.
  • Approval and Disbursal: Typically completed within a few days, with disbursal directly into the applicant's bank account.

7. Fees and Other Charges

  • Processing Fee: 1.5% - 2% of the loan amount + GST.
  • Late Payment Fee: 2% per month on overdue EMI.
  • Prepayment/Foreclosure Charges: 4% of the outstanding principal amount if closed before one year; typically lower if closed after one year.
  • Document Retrieval Charges: ₹500 per document set.

8. Benefits for Axis Bank Account Holders

  • Pre-Approved Loan Offers: For account holders with a good banking history.
  • Lower Interest Rates: Possible concession in interest rates based on the relationship with the bank.
  • Digital Loan Management: Manage loan details via the Axis Bank mobile app or net banking.

9. Additional Information

  • Credit Score Requirement: A credit score of 750 or higher is generally required for favorable terms.
  • Loan Insurance: Optional insurance coverage can protect the borrower’s family from outstanding liabilities in case of unforeseen circumstances.
  • Loan Amount Calculator: Axis Bank provides an online loan eligibility and EMI calculator to help borrowers estimate their loan capacity and monthly repayment amount.
  • Customer Support: Dedicated loan support via customer care, chat, and in-branch assistance.

10. Axis Bank Personal Loan Products

  • Personal Loan for Salaried Individuals: Tailored for salaried employees with steady monthly income.
  • Personal Loan for Self-Employed: Meets the needs of self-employed professionals or non-professionals with varying income sources.
  • Personal Loan for NRIs: Exclusively designed for NRIs with specific eligibility conditions regarding their foreign income and residential status.
