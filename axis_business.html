Axis Bank Business Loan

1. Business Loan Types

  • Term Loans: Medium to long-term loans for purchasing machinery, expanding business infrastructure, or for capital expenditure.
  • Working Capital Loans: Short-term financing options to cover day-to-day operational expenses.
  • Equipment Financing: Specific loans for purchasing machinery and equipment, often secured by the purchased assets.
  • Overdraft Facility: Allows businesses to withdraw funds beyond their account balance, up to a predefined limit.
  • Cash Credit: A revolving credit facility for businesses to meet short-term financial needs.
  • Letter of Credit (LC) and Bank Guarantees: Financing for businesses engaged in import/export, ensuring transactions and payments.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Businesses must be operational for a minimum of 3 years.
  • Annual turnover requirements vary based on the type of loan and amount, typically above ₹30 lakhs for most loan types.
  • Applicant must have a stable credit history with no major defaults.
  • Axis Bank usually requires applicants to have been audited for at least one year.

3. Documentation Required

  • Business and Financial Documents: Balance sheets, profit & loss statements for the last 2-3 years, income tax returns, GST filings, etc.
  • KYC Documents: ID proof (e.g., PAN card, passport), address proof (e.g., utility bills, rental agreements).
  • Ownership Documents: Proof of business ownership, such as partnership deed, company incorporation certificate, GST registration, and bank account statements.
  • Collateral Documents (for secured loans): Papers for the assets offered as collateral, if applicable.

4. Loan Amount and Tenure

  • Loan Amount: Varies based on the business profile, ranging typically from ₹50,000 to ₹5 crore.
  • Tenure: Working capital loans and overdrafts may range from 12-36 months, while term loans can go up to 7 years.

5. Interest Rates and Fees

  • Interest Rates: Starting from approximately 11% and may go up to 18%, depending on the business profile, loan type, and credit score.
  • Processing Fees: Usually between 1-2% of the loan amount.
  • Prepayment Charges: Range from 2-5% if applicable, based on when the loan is prepaid and type of loan.

6. Application Process

  • Step 1: Fill out an application form online or at a branch.
  • Step 2: Submit all required documents, including KYC, financials, and business registration papers.
  • Step 3: Axis Bank assesses creditworthiness, and loan approval may require a site visit and discussions.
  • Step 4: Once approved, funds are disbursed within a few working days, based on loan type and processing time.

7. Unique Benefits

  • Flexi Loans: Axis Bank offers flexi-loan options for certain business loans, allowing partial withdrawals and repayments.
  • Dedicated Relationship Manager: Businesses may receive personalized support from a relationship manager to assist with loan management.
  • Online Loan Management: Access to the loan management portal allows businesses to view outstanding balances, make repayments, and monitor loan usage.

8. Additional Services

  • Axis Bank also provides business advisory services, such as financial planning, investment guidance, and loan structuring to ensure optimal fund usage.
