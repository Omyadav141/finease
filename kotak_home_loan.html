Kotak Mahindra Bank Home Loans

1. Loan Purpose

  • New Home Purchase: Financing for purchasing new residential properties.
  • Home Construction: Funds for constructing a home on owned land.
  • Home Renovation/Improvement: Loans for renovating or upgrading existing properties.
  • Balance Transfer: Transfer an existing home loan from another bank to Kotak for lower interest rates and better terms.
  • Home Loan Plus: Additional funding for personal use against an existing Kotak home loan.

2. Loan Amount

  • Loan amounts typically range from ₹5 Lakhs to ₹10 Crores, depending on eligibility, property value, and repayment capacity.

3. Interest Rates

  • Type: Both fixed and floating interest rates are available.
  • Floating Rate: Starts from 8.75% per annum, based on applicant profile, loan amount, and tenure.
  • Fixed Rate: Typically higher than the floating rate; consult the bank for the latest fixed rate.

4. Loan Tenure

  • Maximum tenure: Up to 20-25 years, depending on the borrower’s age and employment type.

5. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18-60 years for salaried; up to 65 years for self-employed at loan maturity.
  • Income: Minimum net monthly income of ₹20,000 for salaried applicants.
  • Credit Score: A CIBIL score of 700 or above is preferred.
  • Employment Status: Open to both salaried and self-employed professionals.

6. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: PAN, Aadhaar, Passport, or Voter ID.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, Aadhaar, Passport, etc.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Last 3 months’ salary slips, 6 months’ bank statements, Form 16.
    • Self-employed: Last 2 years’ ITR, financial statements, and business proofs.
  • Property Documents: Sale agreement, title deeds, allotment letter, etc.

7. Fees and Charges

  • Processing Fee: 0.5% to 1% of the loan amount.
  • Prepayment Charges: No charges for floating rate loans for individuals; applicable for fixed-rate loans.
  • Other Charges: Stamp duty, valuation, and documentation fees may apply.

8. Special Features

  • Flexible Payment Options: Part-prepayment and EMI holidays available under certain terms.
  • Tax Benefits: Interest and principal repayment may be eligible for deductions under Sections 80C and 24(b).
  • Balance Transfer with Top-Up Loan: Transfer existing loans and avail additional funding.

9. Application Process

  • Offline: Visit a Kotak Mahindra Bank branch with required documents.
  • Online: Apply via Kotak’s website, upload digital documents, and track the application status.
