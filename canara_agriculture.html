Canara Bank Agriculture Loan Options

1. Agricultural DRI Loans

  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹15,000 for agricultural purposes and ₹20,000 for housing purposes.
  • Interest Rates: Simple rate of interest at 4% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: To be repaid within 36 months without any repayment holiday.
  • Features:
    • 100% financing for agricultural activities.
    • Minimal processing charges: 0.30% of the loan amount with a minimum of ₹250 and a maximum of ₹3,000 (GST inclusive).
    • No prepayment penalty for early repayment.
    • Quick approval and disbursement.
    • Suitable for working capital and term loans for agricultural or allied activities.
  • Eligibility: Family income from all sources should not exceed ₹24,000 per annum in urban/semi-urban areas and ₹18,000 per annum in rural areas. Land holding should not exceed 1 acre of irrigated land or 2.5 acres of un-irrigated land. SC/ST borrowers are eligible irrespective of land holdings.

2. Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Loans

  • Loan Amount: Up to ₹3 lakhs.
  • Interest Rates: Starting from 7.5% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure aligned with crop cycles or business operations.
  • Features:
    • 100% financing for agricultural activities.
    • Minimal processing charges: 0.30% of the loan amount with a minimum of ₹250 and a maximum of ₹3,000 (GST inclusive).
    • No prepayment penalty for early repayment.
    • Quick approval and disbursement.
    • Suitable for purchasing seeds, fertilizers, machinery, or expanding farmland.
  • Eligibility: Farmers, agri-business owners, and rural entrepreneurs engaged in agricultural activities.

General Features of Canara Bank Agriculture Loans

  • No income proof needed for loan application.
  • Part payment allowed during the loan tenure.
  • No foreclosure charges after three months of loan disbursal.
  • High loan-to-value (LTV) ratio, up to 90% of agricultural needs.
  • Insurance options available, such as personal accident cover and critical illness cover for a nominal premium.
  • 24/7 customer service for queries and assistance.
  • Online access to loan accounts to track status, repayments, and transactions.
