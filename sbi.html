SBI - Saving Bank Accounts

Open a saving bank account online with SBI and enjoy mobile, internet, and YONO banking features. Choose from various types of savings accounts based on your eligibility and requirements.

Features

Feature Details
Mobile Banking Manage your account from your mobile phone with SBI's mobile banking app.
SMS Alerts Receive instant alerts on your transactions through SMS.
Internet Banking Access your account online and perform various banking transactions.
YONO Use SBI's YONO app for banking and lifestyle services.
State Bank Anywhere Access banking services anywhere with SBI's online platforms.
SBI Quick Missed Call Facility Quick access to account information through missed calls.
Cheque Leaves First 10 cheque leaves free in a financial year. Thereafter, charges apply.
Withdrawals Restricted free withdrawals based on Monthly Average Balance maintained.
Pass Book A passbook is issued to record transactions. Duplicate passbook can be issued if lost.
Account Statement Free consolidated account statement available.
