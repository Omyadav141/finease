  • Indian citizens.

Documentation

To open a savings account online:

  • Aadhaar Number.
  • PAN number.

To open a savings account at branch:

  • Passport-size photograph.
  • PAN Card or filled Form 60.
  • Any one of the following document:
    • Aadhaar Card.
    • Passport.
    • Voter’s Identity Card (Election Card).
    • Permanent and valid driving license with photograph.
    • Job Card issued by NREGA duly signed by an officer of the State Government.
    • Letter issued by the National Population Register containing details of name and address.
