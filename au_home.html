AU Small Finance Bank Home Loan

1. Types of Home Loans

  • Home Purchase Loan: For purchasing ready-to-move or under-construction homes.
  • Home Improvement Loan: For home repairs, renovations, and other upgrades.
  • Home Extension Loan: For expanding the existing property.
  • Plot Loan: For buying a residential plot.
  • Balance Transfer: Transfer an existing home loan from another lender to AU Small Finance Bank at potentially lower rates.

2. Loan Amount

  • Loan Amount: Varies based on applicant’s income, repayment capability, and property value. Limits range from ₹3 lakh to ₹2 crore, with higher amounts possible in specific cases.

3. Interest Rates

  • Interest Rate: Competitive rates offered in both fixed and floating options. Rates typically range from 8.35% to 12%, based on factors like credit score, employment type, and loan amount.

4. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 to 60 years (for salaried) or up to 65 years (for self-employed) at loan maturity.
  • Income: Minimum monthly income of ₹20,000. Higher loan amounts require higher income.
  • Employment: Both salaried individuals and self-employed professionals are eligible.
  • Credit Score: A preferred score of 650 and above. The bank may consider lower scores based on repayment history and financial stability.

5. Loan Tenure

  • Loan Tenure: Flexible loan tenure ranging from 1 to 30 years, providing borrowers with options to lower their EMIs or reduce the total interest over the term.

6. Processing Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Typically 0.5% to 1% of the loan amount, subject to a minimum amount.
  • Foreclosure Charges: No charges for pre-closure on floating-rate loans if paid from own funds. Charges may apply for fixed-rate loans.
  • Late Payment Fees: Around 2% per month on the overdue EMI.
  • Legal and Technical Fees: Charges applicable for property verification and legal processing.

7. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, etc.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, Aadhaar, passport, etc.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Salary slips for the last 3 months, Form 16, and bank statements for the past 6 months.
    • Self-Employed: Income tax returns for the last 2-3 years, business financials, and bank statements for the last year.
  • Property Documents: Title deeds, property sale agreement, NOC from the builder (for under-construction properties), and other relevant documents.
  • Other Documents: Passport-size photos, application form, and any required declarations.

8. Special Features

  • Flexible EMI Options: Choose step-up or step-down EMIs based on your needs.
  • Top-Up Loan Facility: Available for existing home loan customers needing additional funds.
  • Quick Approval: AU Small Finance Bank focuses on quick processing for eligible customers.

9. Application Process

  • Online Application: Apply through the AU Small Finance Bank website or mobile app by submitting necessary details and documents.
  • Offline Application: Visit any branch for assistance throughout the loan application process.
  • Eligibility Confirmation: Eligibility and loan amount are confirmed post-document verification and credit appraisal.
