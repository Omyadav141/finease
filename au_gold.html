AU Small Finance Bank Gold Loan

1. Loan Features and Benefits

  • Loan Amount: Based on the weight and purity of gold provided, varying with market gold rates.
  • Interest Rates: Competitive rates starting around 8-10% annually, depending on loan terms.
  • Tenure: Flexible tenure, ranging from a few months to 12-24 months.
  • Repayment Options: EMI, bullet repayment (interest-only payments with principal at the end), and overdraft facilities.
  • Processing Fees: A nominal fee between 0.5% and 1% of the loan amount.
  • Disbursement Speed: Fast disbursement, often within the same day as the loan is secured by gold.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Requirement: Applicants should be between 18 and 70 years old.
  • Loan Applicant Profile: Salaried employees, self-employed, farmers, and traders can apply.
  • Gold Quality: Only 18-24 karat gold ornaments are accepted; bars and coins are generally not eligible.

3. Documentation Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN card, voter ID, passport, or driving license.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, ration card, bank statement, or passport.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photos.
  • Additional Documents: Signed loan application and any forms required for compliance or regulatory purposes.

4. Key Benefits

  • Quick Loan Processing: Minimal paperwork and quick turnaround time.
  • High Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio: Up to 75-90% of the gold’s market value.
  • Secured Loan: A secured loan offering flexibility in repayment without heavy penalties.
  • No Income Proof Required: Income documents are not needed, making the loan accessible to individuals without regular income.
  • Gold Security and Safety: Gold ornaments are securely stored by the bank during the loan tenure.

5. Fees and Charges

  • Processing Fee: Typically between 0.5% and 1% of the loan amount.
  • Prepayment Charges: Minimal or nil, but some charges may apply for early loan repayment.
  • Late Payment Penalties: Applicable if payments are not made on time.

6. Application Process

  • Branch Visit: Visit the nearest AU Small Finance Bank branch with your gold ornaments, documents, and complete the loan application form.
  • Evaluation and Disbursement: The gold's purity is evaluated, loan amount determined, and funds disbursed directly to the applicant’s bank account.
