ICICI Bank Advantage Woman Savings Account

The Advantage Woman Savings Account is specifically designed to cater to the banking needs of women, offering a range of features and benefits tailored to enhance your financial well-being.

Account Opening Process

Accounts can be opened online via the ICICI Bank portal or offline at a branch. For online applications, submit the required documents and complete the KYC process. Offline applications may require 2-5 business days for verification and activation.
