Kotak Mahindra Agriculture Loan

1. Types of Agriculture Loans

  • Crop Loans: For financing seasonal crop production.
  • Livestock Loans: Financing for purchasing or maintaining livestock.
  • Agri Equipment Loans: Loans for purchasing agricultural machinery like tractors and harvesters.
  • Agri Infrastructure Loans: Loans for building farm infrastructure such as warehouses and cold storage.
  • Agri Business Loans: For farmers looking to set up or expand agri-business ventures.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: Applicants should be between 21-65 years of age.
  • Land Ownership: The borrower should either own or lease agricultural land for farming.
  • Income Proof: Required to demonstrate a stable income, such as income tax returns or financial statements.
  • Business Continuity: Farmers or agribusinesses should have at least 2-3 years of experience in farming or agri-business.

3. Loan Features

  • Loan Amount: Kotak offers agriculture loans from ₹50,000 to ₹2 crore, depending on the loan type and the applicant’s financial profile.
  • Loan Tenure: Loans can be repaid over a period of 6 months to 5 years, depending on the loan type and purpose.
  • Interest Rates: Interest rates for agriculture loans generally range from 8% to 12% per annum, based on the loan type and eligibility.
  • Collateral: Many loans may require collateral, such as agricultural land, machinery, or produce.

4. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, Passport, or Aadhaar card with the correct address.
  • Land Ownership Proof: Land title document or lease agreement.
  • Income Proof: Financial documents such as tax returns, profit and loss statement, etc.
  • Photographs: Passport-sized photographs of the applicant.

5. Application Process

  • Online Application: Apply online through Kotak’s website by submitting the necessary details and documents.
  • Branch Visit: Alternatively, visit a nearby Kotak branch where a representative will assist in processing your application.
  • Loan Assessment: Kotak will evaluate the applicant’s eligibility and the property value before sanctioning the loan.

6. Additional Features

  • Quick Disbursement: Agriculture loans are processed quickly, with funds disbursed promptly.
  • Flexible Repayment Plans: Kotak offers flexible repayment options based on the loan type, ensuring that payments are manageable for farmers.
  • Loan Top-Up: Existing agriculture loan holders may be eligible for a top-up based on repayment history.
