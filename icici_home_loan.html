ICICI Bank Home Loans

Home Loan

Loan Type Details
Loan Amount Up to ₹5 crores.
Interest Rates Starting from 8.75% per annum.
Repayment Tenure Flexible tenure ranging from 12 to 30 years.
Features
  • No Foreclosure Charges: No pre-closure charges if 12 EMIs are paid.
  • Minimal Documentation: Requires minimal documentation, streamlining the application process.
  • Fixed Interest Rates: Benefit from stable and predictable interest rates.
  • No Collateral Required: Secure a home loan without the need to pledge collateral.
  • Flexible Tenure: Choose a repayment tenure that suits your financial situation and goals.
  • Usage: Suitable for purchasing a new home, home renovation, or construction.
Eligibility Salaried individuals with a regular monthly income and a salary account with any bank in India.

Home Loan Balance Transfer

Loan Type Details
Loan Amount Up to ₹5 crores.
Interest Rates Starting from 8.75% per annum.
Repayment Tenure Flexible tenure ranging from 12 to 30 years.
Features
  • Balance Transfer: Transfer your existing home loan to ICICI Bank for lower EMIs.
  • Attractive Interest Rates: Enjoy competitive interest rates on the transferred loan amount.
  • Flexible Tenure: The repayment tenure can be customized to suit your financial situation and goals.
  • No Collateral Required: Similar to the primary home loan, no collateral is needed to avail the balance transfer facility.
  • Usage: Ideal for reducing your loan servicing costs and improving cash flow by transferring high-cost loans to ICICI Bank.
Eligibility Existing home loan borrowers looking to transfer their loan balance to ICICI Bank.
