Bank of India (BOI) Car Loans

1. Loan Amount

  • Up to 85%-90% of the on-road price of the car (new or used).
  • For used cars, loan amounts go up to ₹10 lakh.

2. Interest Rates

  • Starting from 9% to 12% p.a., depending on the applicant’s credit profile.

3. Repayment Tenure

  • Flexible tenure of up to 7 years.

4. Features

  • No prepayment charges after the lock-in period.
  • Quick disbursal, typically within a few working days.
  • Loans available for both new and used cars (subject to conditions).
  • Processing fee: Typically 1% of the loan amount.
  • Flexible EMI options with customizable tenure.
  • Option to transfer the loan to another bank.

5. Eligibility

  • Salaried individuals, self-employed individuals, and business owners.
  • Age: Minimum 21 years; Maximum age at loan maturity: 60 years.
  • Income: Minimum ₹25,000/month for salaried employees.
  • Good credit score (preferably 650 or above).

6. Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID, etc.
  • Proof of Residence: Utility bills, rental agreement, etc.
  • Proof of Income: Salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • Car quotation and vehicle registration details.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
