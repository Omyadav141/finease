Axis Bank Agriculture Loan

1. Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

  • Purpose: To meet the short-term credit requirements for crop cultivation, post-harvest expenses, and contingencies.
  • Eligibility: Farmers, tenant farmers, sharecroppers, and self-help groups (SHGs) involved in agriculture.
  • Loan Amount: Varies based on crop type, area under cultivation, and estimated yield. Typically up to ₹3 lakh.
  • Interest Rate: Interest subsidy may be available under government schemes.
  • Repayment: Based on crop harvesting period (usually 6-12 months).
  • Documentation Required: Identity proof, address proof, land ownership proof, and income proof.
  • Benefits: Flexible loan facility with renewals, easy access to working capital, interest subvention scheme eligibility.

2. Agri Equipment Loan

  • Purpose: Financing for purchasing tractors, harvesters, and other agricultural equipment.
  • Eligibility: Individual farmers, Joint Liability Groups (JLGs), and SHGs.
  • Loan Amount: Based on equipment cost, margin money requirements, and borrower’s eligibility.
  • Interest Rate: Competitive rates; varies depending on the type of equipment and borrower profile.
  • Repayment: Tenure typically ranges from 3 to 7 years.
  • Documents Required: KYC, proof of landholding, equipment quotation, and income proof.
  • Benefits: Customizable EMI options, financing up to 85% of equipment value.

3. Agricultural Term Loans

  • Purpose: For setting up infrastructure like irrigation, horticulture, farm mechanization, etc.
  • Eligibility: Farmers, individual landowners, and entrepreneurs in agri-allied sectors.
  • Loan Amount: Based on project cost and repayment capacity.
  • Interest Rate: Varies with loan amount and tenure.
  • Repayment: Up to 7 years with flexible EMI or repayment options.
  • Documents Required: Project report, landholding details, income proof, and KYC documents.
  • Benefits: Lower interest rates for long-term agri-investments, repayment aligned with cash flow.

4. Warehouse Receipt Financing

  • Purpose: To help farmers get credit by leveraging stored agricultural produce as collateral.
  • Eligibility: Farmers and traders holding agricultural produce in accredited warehouses.
  • Loan Amount: Typically, 70-80% of the value of stored produce.
  • Interest Rate: Competitive and varies by tenure.
  • Repayment: Short-term, linked to produce sale period.
  • Documents Required: Warehouse receipt, stock details, KYC, and income proof.
  • Benefits: Quick processing, credit availability post-harvest, avoids distress selling.

5. Contract Farming Loans

  • Purpose: For financing farmers who have contractual agreements to supply produce to buyers.
  • Eligibility: Farmers with a valid contract with buyers or companies.
  • Loan Amount: Depends on contract value and input costs.
  • Interest Rate: Market-linked, varies by contract type.
  • Repayment: Flexible as per contract cycle and produce sale.
  • Documents Required: Contract documents, KYC, income proof, landholding proof.
  • Benefits: Tailored financing options based on contract terms, working capital availability.

6. Dairy and Poultry Loans

  • Purpose: For supporting dairy, poultry, and livestock farming businesses.
  • Eligibility: Farmers and business owners involved in dairy, poultry, and animal husbandry.
  • Loan Amount: Based on project cost and repayment capacity.
  • Interest Rate: Competitive rates tailored for dairy and poultry sectors.
  • Repayment: Flexible repayment options, typically in line with cash flow cycles.
  • Documents Required: KYC, project report, income proof, and proof of operations.
  • Benefits: Longer tenure for asset purchase, working capital for operations.

7. Horticulture Loans

  • Purpose: To fund activities like floriculture, fruits, and vegetables farming.
  • Eligibility: Individual farmers and agribusiness entities.
  • Loan Amount: Project-based, with a high loan-to-cost ratio.
  • Interest Rate: Varies, with specific schemes for horticulture.
  • Repayment: Flexible repayment aligned with crop cycles.
  • Documents Required: KYC, income proof, project report, and land ownership.
  • Benefits: Dedicated financing for high-value horticulture activities, income-aligned EMI options.
