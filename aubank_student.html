About AU Student Savings Account

Regardless of age, understanding the value of money and the importance of financial stability is an essential life skill. This is even more true for hardworking students, who aim for the stars and are looking for ways to achieve their goals.

Our AU Student Account helps you do exactly this. Offering high interest rates, monthly interest payouts, and no minimum balance requirement, our Student Account can help you create a stable financial future so that you can achieve every goal you set your sights on.

Your educational institution needs to be empaneled with us for you to be able to open an AU Student Savings Account.

Features of an AU Student Account

Features

• High-interest rates: Earn up to 7.25%* p.a. on your Savings Bank Account & receive monthly interest payouts. For details, visit Savings Account Interest Rates.

• Monthly interest payouts: Receive monthly interest payouts to manage your cash flows better.

• Banking at your convenience: No need to visit a branch; now get all your banking transactions & other service requests executed from the comfort of your home with the help of our Video Banking facilities.

• Domestic lounge access: Our power-packed Debit Card offers complimentary access to domestic airport lounges. (Effective 1st July 2024, complimentary lounge access benefit given to RuPay Platinum International Debit Card is withdrawn.)

• State-of-the-art Digital Banking: Enjoy 24x7 access to your funds through AU 0101 App or AU 0101 NetBanking. What’s more, you can even book flights, hotels, recharge your mobile, and apply for a host of products and services.

Benefits

• Feature-rich Debit Card: Get a RuPay Debit Card that provides purchase protection and offers along with discounts across leading brands in India.

• Accessibility: Now, access your account with our branch and ATM network across the country. We even offer complimentary access to other bank ATMs.

• Paperless banking: Enjoy the convenience of depositing funds into your account without the hassle of filling in a deposit slip.

• Easy deposit facility: Now begin your wealth journey by parking idle funds in our high interest-bearing Fixed Deposits.

Service Fees for an AU Student Account

We believe in complete transparency and provide you with a list of charges for the services we offer. These include charges for your Debit Card, payment and collection services, transactions, and delivery fees. For more details, please refer to our Service Fee Reckoner.

Eligibility Criteria

If you are a resident of India and are pursuing a course at a school, college, or university, you are eligible to open an AU Student Account with us. However, if you are a minor, you should have a parent or guardian who can operate the account till you turn 18.
