SBI - Term Deposit

Park your lump sum amount in an SBI Term Deposit and avail of features like guaranteed returns, choice of interest pay-out, and liquidity through OD or premature withdrawal. Invest today through Online-SBI or by visiting your nearest branch.

Feature Details

Interest Payment Quarterly from the date of issue or at maturity. Monthly, half-yearly, or yearly intervals upon request for terms of twelve months and above.
Nomination Available in favor of individual only
Transferability Allowed among branches
Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) Applicable as per Income Tax Rules. Form 15G/15H can be submitted for exemption from tax deduction.

>36 months & up to 60 months: 10% margin
>60 months: 15% margin

Most Important Terms and Conditions

  • Interest can be paid on monthly intervals at a discounted rate.
  • Interest can also be paid on a calendar quarter basis.
  • TDS at prevalent Income Tax rate is deducted if Form 15G/15H is not submitted.
