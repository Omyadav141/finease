Bank of India (BOI) Agriculture Loans

1. Loan Types

  • Kisan Credit Card (KCC)
  • Agricultural Term Loan
  • Farm Equipment Loan
  • Dairy and Poultry Loans
  • Crop Loan

2. Loan Amount

  • Kisan Credit Card: Up to ₹3 lakh (non-collateralized loans).
  • Agricultural Term Loan: Covers up to 80% of project cost.
  • Farm Equipment Loan: Usually up to ₹25 lakh or more.
  • Dairy/Poultry Loans: Up to ₹50 lakh, based on project requirements.

3. Interest Rates

  • Kisan Credit Card: 7% to 9% p.a. (interest subvention available).
  • Agricultural Term Loan: 8% to 9% p.a.
  • Farm Equipment Loan: 8% to 10% p.a.
  • Dairy and Poultry Loans: 8% to 10% p.a.

4. Repayment Tenure

  • Kisan Credit Card: Up to 3 years with seasonal repayment linked to crop cycles.
  • Agricultural Term Loan: Up to 7 years.
  • Farm Equipment Loan: Up to 7 years.
  • Dairy/Poultry Loans: Generally 3 to 5 years, based on project cash flow.

5. Features

  • Flexible repayment based on crop cycles.
  • Low interest rates with government subsidies.
  • Loans for machinery purchase and farm expansion.
  • Eligible for crop and asset insurance.
  • Backed by government agricultural schemes.

6. Eligibility

  • Farmers with land holdings (individual or joint).
  • Minimum age: 18 years; Maximum age: 65 years at loan maturity.
  • For KCC: Proof of landholding, income, and prior farming experience required.
  • For term loans: Project feasibility reports may be required.

7. Documents Required

  • Proof of land ownership or lease (if applicable).
  • Crop and farming details.
  • Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID).
  • Income proof (e.g., salary slips, ITR).
  • Land records and bank account details.
