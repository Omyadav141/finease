Bank of India (BOI) Home Loans

1. Loan Amount

  • For Purchase: Up to ₹10 crore for house or flat.
  • For Construction/Renovation: Loan amount based on estimated construction cost or market value.
  • Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio: Up to 90% (depending on loan amount and applicant’s profile).

2. Interest Rates

  • Starting from 8.35% to 9.25% p.a. (subject to market conditions and applicant's profile).
  • Floating Rate: Most loans are based on this rate.
  • Special Benefit: Women applicants may get an additional 0.05% interest rate discount.

3. Repayment Tenure

  • Maximum tenure of up to 30 years.

4. Features

  • Prepayment: No prepayment charges for repaying the loan early.
  • Flexible EMI: Choose between monthly or quarterly payments.
  • Tax Benefits: Eligible for tax deductions under Section 80C (principal) and Section 24 (interest).
  • Top-Up Loans: Available for existing borrowers to enhance the loan amount.

5. Eligibility

  • Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and NRIs are eligible.
  • Age: Minimum 21 years at loan initiation, maximum 60-65 years at loan maturity.
  • Income: Minimum monthly income of ₹25,000 or higher for salaried employees.
  • Credit Score: A good score of 750 or above is preferred.
  • Co-Applicants: Can include family members, spouse, or children.

6. Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, or Voter ID.
  • Address Proof: Utility bill, rental agreement, or passport.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salary Slips (for salaried employees).
    • Income Tax Returns and Bank Statements (for self-employed professionals).
  • Property Documents: Sale deed, construction estimate, etc.
