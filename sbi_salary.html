SBI - Digi Salary Account

Features

Feature Details
Eligibility Only Resident India Individuals over 18 years of age with capacity to contract in accordance with applicable laws in India, without any tax liability outside India.
Account Type Only one SBI Digital Savings Account per individual. Joint accounts or joint mandates are not permitted.
Conversion Account can be converted to a joint account or CSP Account only after converting it to a normal savings account by visiting the home branch.
Platform Availability Available on iPhone with iOS 8 and above, Android Phones with Android version 5.0 and above.
Nomination Facility Available within the Yono by SBI app and is mandatory for Digital Savings Account. Only one nominee allowed.
Account Closure Can be closed by making a written request at the home branch only.
Cheque Book Charges will be the same as applicable to normal savings accounts.
Debit Card A Classic Debit Card will be issued free of cost. ATM pin has to be generated through Green PIN process. Annual maintenance charges as per normal savings accounts.
Passbook Not provided. Audio-visual statement sent by email.
Electronic Statement Available monthly, as per Bank's record, subject to discretion of the Bank.
Minimum Balance To be maintained as defined for normal Savings Bank Account. Minimum balance charges waived till 31st March 2019.

Eligibility

Eligibility Criteria Details
Age Over 18 years
Identification Valid Aadhaar number and Permanent Account Number (PAN). Name on Aadhaar should match the PAN card.
Contact Information Valid and active local mobile number and email address.
e-KYC Completion of e-KYC through biometric authentication at any SBI branch.
Account Limitations One SBI Digital Savings Account per individual. One account per Mobile Phone/Device.
