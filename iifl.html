Charges and Fees

Account Opening Charges

Fee: One-time fee for opening a demat account. Details: May vary based on account type and additional services.

Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC)

Fee: Annual fee for maintaining the demat account. Details: Can be a flat fee or based on the number of holdings.

Transaction Charges

Fee: Charges per transaction when buying or selling securities. Details: May vary based on the volume of transactions and the type of security.

Dematerialization Charges

Fee: Charges for converting physical securities into electronic form. Details: Can be a per-security fee or based on the number of certificates.

Rematerialization Charges

Fee: Charges for converting electronic securities back into physical form. Details: Typically charged per certificate or security.

Custodian Charges

Fee: Charges for the safekeeping of securities. Details: May be included in the AMC or billed separately.

Service Charges

Fee: Charges for additional services such as handling corporate actions (e.g., rights issues, bonus shares). Details: Can be a flat fee or based on the type of corporate action.

Handling Charges

Fee: Charges for processing requests like transfer of securities, change of address, etc. Details: Can be billed per request or transaction.

Late Fees

Fee: Charges for delayed payments or penalties. Details: Applied if there are delays in payment of fees or charges.

Other Miscellaneous Charges

Fee: Any other fees that may apply, such as for account inactivity or specific administrative services.
