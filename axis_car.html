Axis Bank Car Loan

1. Loan Amount & Coverage

  • Loan Amount: Up to 100% of the car's on-road price for select borrowers.
  • Minimum Loan Amount: Generally INR 1 lakh, but varies based on the car model and borrower profile.
  • Maximum Loan Amount: Based on applicant profile, car value, and credit history.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21 to 65 years.
  • Employment:
    • Salaried: Minimum monthly income requirement of INR 15,000.
    • Self-employed: Minimum annual income requirement of INR 1.8 lakhs.
  • Credit Score: A CIBIL score of 700+ is typically preferred.

3. Interest Rates

  • Range: Approximately 7.95% to 9.10% p.a., depending on the borrower’s profile and loan tenure.
  • Type: Fixed or floating rate options are generally offered.

4. Loan Tenure

  • Repayment Period: Up to 7 years (84 months), with flexible tenure options to reduce EMI burden.

5. Processing Fees & Charges

  • Processing Fee: Usually 1% of the loan amount (with a minimum cap).
  • Foreclosure Charges: Generally, around 5% if closed within the first 6 months; charges may reduce with time.
  • Late Payment Fees: 2% per month on overdue EMI.
  • Part-Payment: Allowed after 6 months in some cases, with charges applicable.

6. Documents Required

  • Personal Documents: Proof of identity (Aadhaar, PAN), proof of age, proof of address.
  • Income Documents:
    • Salaried: Salary slips, Form 16, bank statements.
    • Self-employed: Income Tax Returns, financial statements, bank statements.
  • Vehicle Documents: Proforma invoice of the car, registration certificate, insurance documents (for used cars).

7. Special Features and Benefits

  • Pre-approved Offers: Existing Axis Bank customers may receive pre-approved offers with faster processing.
  • Online Application & Tracking: Available for easy tracking.
  • Flexible EMI Options: Step-up or balloon EMI options available to suit income flow.
  • Instant E-Approval: Often provided for eligible customers for quicker disbursement.

8. Application Process

  • Step 1: Submit an application online or visit an Axis Bank branch.
  • Step 2: Provide necessary documents and choose loan tenure.
  • Step 3: Bank conducts eligibility assessment and document verification.
  • Step 4: Loan sanction and agreement signing, followed by disbursement.

9. Other Considerations

  • Used Car Loans: Available, with up to 90% of the car’s valuation financed.
  • Refinancing Options: Available for existing car owners looking to refinance their vehicle.
