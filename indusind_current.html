IndusInd Bank - Current Account

Discover the benefits and features of IndusInd Bank's Current Account for businesses and professionals.

Key Features

Free Debit Card Issued along with the account for easy access to funds and transactions.
Cheque Book Free personalized cheque book provided for easy payments.
Online Banking Access to online banking for transactions, account management, and bill payments.
Overdraft Facility Available based on credit evaluation for additional funds when needed.
Fund Transfers Free fund transfers within India for seamless transactions.
Cash Deposits Unlimited cash deposits at branch counters and ATMs.

Eligibility

Sole proprietorships, partnerships, private limited companies, public limited companies, and professionals are eligible to open an IndusInd Bank Current Account.

Documents Required

  • Business Registration Documents (Certificate of Incorporation, Partnership Deed, etc.)
  • Proof of Identity and Address (PAN card, Aadhaar card, Passport, Utility bill, etc.)
  • Proof of Business Address (Lease agreement, utility bill, etc.)
  • KYC Documents for authorized signatories

Fees and Charges

  • Minimum Balance Requirements: Varies based on the type of current account and location.
  • Non-Maintenance Charges: Fees may apply if the minimum balance is not maintained.
  • Transaction Charges: Charges for transactions beyond a certain limit.
