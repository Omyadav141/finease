General Features of Canara Bank Education Loans

  • No income proof required for loan application.
  • Option for part payment before the loan tenure ends.
  • No foreclosure charges after three months.
  • High loan-to-value (LTV) ratio: Up to 90% of the course fee.
  • Insurance options including personal accident and critical illness cover for a nominal premium.
  • 24/7 customer service to assist with any inquiries.
  • Online access to manage your loan status, repayment schedules, and transaction history.
