IndusInd Bank - Student Account

The IndusInd Bank Student Account is designed to meet the specific banking needs of students, providing essential features and services to manage finances efficiently.

Key Features

Zero Balance Account No requirement to maintain a minimum balance.
ATM Card Complimentary debit card with moderate withdrawal limits for safe spending.
Interest Rates Interest calculated daily and credited monthly on the savings balance.
Online Banking 24x7 access to IndusNet for fund transfers, balance checks, and payments.
Mobile Banking Manage finances on the go with the Indus Mobile App, including fund transfers and bill payments.
ATM Access Free withdrawals at IndusInd Bank ATMs, with limited free transactions at non-IndusInd ATMs.
Cheque Book Personalized cheque book may be issued if needed, although online methods are preferred.
Free SMS Alerts Receive SMS alerts for all transactions to stay informed about account activity.
Discounts and Offers Special offers and discounts on shopping, dining, and entertainment.
Education Loans Assistance with student loans for higher education.

Eligibility

  • Age: Typically 18 to 25 years old.
  • Student Status: Must be a student at a recognized educational institution.
  • Indian Resident: Must be an Indian resident.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity: Aadhaar card, Passport, PAN card, Voter ID.
  • Proof of Address: Aadhaar, utility bills, or rental agreement.
  • Student ID Card: Valid student ID card or bonafide certificate from the educational institution.
  • Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs (usually 1-2).

Fees and Charges

  • Maintenance Fees: Typically none due to zero-balance requirement.
  • ATM Charges: Unlimited free transactions at IndusInd Bank ATMs, with a limited number of free transactions at non-IndusInd ATMs. Nominal fees may apply beyond free limits.
  • Debit Card Charges: Standard debit card is often free; premium cards may incur charges.
  • Online Transaction Charges: Free for online banking services such as fund transfers and bill payments.

Benefits

  • Financial Independence: Manage finances independently and learn basic banking and budgeting skills.
  • Safety and Security: Includes safety features like transaction alerts and secure online banking.
  • Convenience: Hassle-free management of funds, bill payments, and online shopping.
  • Savings: Zero-balance requirement means no penalties and more savings.
  • Access to Other Banking Services: Access to education loans, fixed deposits, and recurring deposits.

Additional Services

  • Education Loans: Easy application for student loans for higher studies.
  • Fixed and Recurring Deposits: Options for saving with higher interest rates.
  • Parental Access: Some accounts may allow joint control by parents or guardians.

How to Open a Student Account

  • Visit a Branch: Go to the nearest IndusInd Bank branch with required documents.
  • Online Application: Some branches may allow online applications where you can fill in your details and upload documents via the bank’s website.
