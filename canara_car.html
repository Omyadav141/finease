Canara Bank Car Loan Options

1. New Vehicle Loan

  • Loan Amount: No upper limit.
  • Interest Rates: Starting from 8.50% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: Maximum period of 84 months.
  • Features:
    • Up to 90% financing for new vehicles.
    • Processing charges: 0.25% on loan amount (min ₹1,000, max ₹5,000 including GST).
    • Reducing balance interest rate (calculated on outstanding balance).
    • No pre-payment penalty.
    • Quick approval for pre-approved customers.
    • Suitable for purchasing new cars, including electric vehicles.
  • Eligibility: Indian nationals aged between 18 and 70 years with a confirmed job and salary account with Canara Bank.

2. Pre-owned Vehicle Loan

  • Loan Amount: Maximum of ₹50 lakhs.
  • Interest Rates: Starting from 8.50% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: Maximum period of 60 months.
  • Features:
    • Up to 60% financing for pre-owned vehicles (not older than 3 years).
    • Processing charges: 0.25% on loan amount (min ₹1,000, max ₹5,000 including GST).
    • Reducing balance interest rate (calculated on outstanding balance).
    • No pre-payment penalty.
    • Quick approval for pre-approved customers.
    • Suitable for purchasing pre-owned cars (not older than 3 years).
  • Eligibility: Indian nationals aged between 18 and 70 years with a confirmed job and salary account with Canara Bank.

General Features of Canara Bank Car Loans

  • No income proof required for loan application.
  • Option for part payment before the loan tenure ends.
  • No foreclosure charges after three months.
  • High loan-to-value (LTV) ratio: Up to 90% for new vehicles and 60% for pre-owned vehicles.
  • Insurance options including personal accident and critical illness cover for a nominal premium.
  • 24/7 customer service to assist with any inquiries.
  • Online access to manage your loan status, repayment schedules, and transaction history.
