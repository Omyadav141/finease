HDFC Kids Savings Account

Save for your child’s future while teaching them to manage money. You can build a future fund with just ₹1,000 a month.

Features

Feature Description
Secured Future Free Education Insurance cover of ₹1,00,000/- in the event of the death of the parent/guardian due to a vehicular accident.
Standing Instruction Transfer any amount from your account to the Kid's Advantage account every month (Minimum value ₹1,000 & Minimum tenure 1 year).
MoneyMaximizer Transfer excess amount to a Fixed Deposit for 1 year and 1 day if the balance exceeds ₹35,000.
Bundling of Sukanya Samriddhi Available for a girl child.
My Passion Fund Flexible deposit experience with small savings starting from ₹1,000.
Systematic Investment Plan Invest small amounts on a weekly or monthly basis in Mutual Funds linked to the Kids Savings Account.
Debit Card ATM/International Debit Card issued in your child's name, with a daily withdrawal limit of ₹2,500 and spending limit of ₹10,000.
Transaction Facilities Free cash withdrawals, balance enquiries, and Payable-at-Par cheque book.

Eligibility Criteria

Category
Minor (up to 18 years of age)
Parent/Guardian with an HDFC Bank Savings Account. If not, one needs to be opened first.
