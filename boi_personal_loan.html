Bank of India (BOI) Personal Loans

1. Loan Amount

  • Up to ₹10 lakh (based on eligibility and repayment capacity).

2. Interest Rates

  • Starting from 10.25% to 14% p.a.
  • Rates depend on the applicant’s profile and income level.

3. Repayment Tenure

  • Flexible tenure ranging from 12 to 60 months.

4. Features

  • No collateral required.
  • Fast processing with quick disbursal of funds.
  • Top-Up Loan: Existing customers can avail additional funding.
  • Can be used for weddings, vacations, medical emergencies, home renovations, and more.
  • Processing Fee: Typically 1% of the loan amount (minimum charges apply).
  • Prepayment/Foreclosure: Allowed with nominal charges after a certain period.

5. Eligibility

  • Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and business owners.
  • Age: Between 21 to 58 years (varies for different categories).
  • Minimum Monthly Income: ₹25,000 for salaried employees.
  • Credit Score: Minimum of 650 (preferably 750+ for better approval chances).
  • Stable employment and income required.

6. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.
  • Address Proof: Utility bills, rental agreement, etc.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salary Slips for salaried employees.
    • Income Tax Returns and Bank Statements for self-employed professionals.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
