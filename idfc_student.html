IDFC FIRST Bank - Student Account

The IDFC FIRST Bank Student Account is tailored to meet the needs of young students, providing them with a platform to manage their finances efficiently while gaining financial literacy.

Features

  • No Minimum Balance Requirement: Maintain a zero-balance account without any monthly charges.
  • Free Debit Card: Receive a free debit card with no annual fee.
  • Online and Mobile Banking: Access your account through our secure online and mobile banking platforms.
  • Student Discounts: Enjoy exclusive discounts and offers on various products and services.
  • Financial Literacy Program: Participate in programs designed to enhance your financial knowledge and skills.

Eligibility

  • Indian citizens
  • Students currently enrolled in an educational institution
  • Age between 16 to 25 years
