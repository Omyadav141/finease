Kotak Mahindra Car Loan

1. Loan Amount & Eligibility

  • Loan Amount: Kotak finances up to 90%-100% of the car's on-road price for new cars. A lower percentage is provided for used cars, depending on the vehicle's age and condition.
  • Salaried Individuals: Must be between 21-60 years old with a minimum net monthly income as per Kotak's criteria.
  • Self-Employed Individuals & Business Owners: Must be between 21-65 years old with a stable business record.
  • Documentation: Basic ID proof, address proof, income proof (such as salary slips or ITR), bank statements, and vehicle-related documents.

2. Interest Rates

  • Competitive Rates: Interest rates generally range from 7.25% to 12% annually, depending on factors like customer profile, loan tenure, and the car's value.

3. Tenure & EMI Options

  • Tenure: Loan repayment terms range from 12 to 84 months, allowing for flexible repayment options.
  • EMI Options: Kotak offers EMI calculators online to determine monthly payments based on loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

4. Processing Fees & Other Charges

  • Processing Fees: Typically ranges between 0.5% - 2% of the loan amount, based on the borrower's profile.
  • Foreclosure & Prepayment Charges: Charges may apply for early loan closure, generally around 5-6% on the outstanding principal, though partial prepayments may have different fees.
  • Other Fees: Includes late payment fees, legal fees (if applicable), and stamp duty charges as per state regulations.

5. Features & Benefits

  • Pre-approved Loans: Existing Kotak customers may receive pre-approved offers with simplified documentation.
  • Flexible Repayment Options: Choose a suitable repayment tenure and EMI amount based on your financial situation.
  • Quick Approval: Faster disbursement process, with approvals potentially within hours for eligible customers.
  • Insurance Tie-ups: Kotak offers bundled car insurance options for added convenience.
