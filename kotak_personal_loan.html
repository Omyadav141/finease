Kotak Mahindra Bank Personal Loans

1. Loan Amount and Eligibility

  • Loan Amount: ₹50,000 to ₹25 lakh, based on eligibility and creditworthiness.
  • Eligibility Criteria:
    • Salaried Individuals: Employees of public/private companies, minimum monthly income of ₹25,000.
    • Self-employed Professionals: Income and turnover as per Kotak's guidelines.
    • Age: 21-58 years for salaried individuals; up to 65 years for self-employed.
    • Minimum Employment/Business Tenure: 1 year, with at least 6 months in the current job or business.

2. Loan Tenure

  • Repayment Period: Flexible tenure options between 12 and 60 months.

3. Interest Rates

  • Interest Rate Range: Starting from 10.99% per annum, based on credit score, loan amount, and applicant profile.
  • Type: Fixed interest rate, ensuring constant EMIs.

4. Processing Fees and Charges

  • Processing Fee: 2.0% to 3.0% of the loan amount, plus applicable GST.
  • Foreclosure Charges: 5% of the principal outstanding after a 12-month lock-in period.
  • Late Payment Charges: 2% per month on overdue EMI.
  • Other Charges: EMI bounce charges, loan statement fees, and document retrieval charges may apply.

5. Documentation Required

  • KYC Documents: PAN, Aadhaar, or valid government-issued ID.
  • Address Proof: Aadhaar, utility bill, voter ID, etc.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Last 3 months' salary slips and bank statements.
    • Self-employed: Financial statements, ITR for the last 2 years, and bank statements.
  • Additional Documents: Employment/business proof, professional qualifications for self-employed professionals.

6. Special Features and Benefits

  • Quick Processing: Instant approval with minimal paperwork.
  • Flexible Repayment: Multiple tenure options to fit individual plans.
  • No Collateral Required: Unsecured loan with no need for collateral or guarantor.
  • Digital Application Process: Complete application online, including e-KYC.
  • Pre-approved Offers: Special offers with lower interest rates for Kotak Bank customers.

7. How to Apply

  • Online: Through Kotak's official website or mobile banking app.
  • Offline: Visit a Kotak Mahindra Bank branch.
  • Customer Service: Guidance available for eligibility, documentation, and application status.
