IndusInd Bank - Salary Account

Explore the key features, account types, and benefits of IndusInd Bank's Salary Account offerings.

Key Features

Zero Balance Requirement No minimum balance required for salary account holders.
Interest Rates Competitive interest rates calculated daily and credited monthly.
Free ATM Transactions Unlimited withdrawals at IndusInd ATMs; a set number at non-IndusInd ATMs.
Debit Card Complimentary card with reward points and higher transaction limits.
Online Banking Full access to internet and mobile banking for fund transfers and payments.
Quick Loan Approvals Pre-approved loans and credit cards with faster processing.

Types of IndusInd Bank Salary Accounts

  • Indus Prestige Salary Account: Premium services like a Platinum Debit Card, unlimited ATM withdrawals, and preferential loan rates.
  • Indus Comfort Salary Account: Zero balance, free debit card, and full access to digital banking.
  • Indus Value Salary Account: No balance requirement, free cheque book, and easy access to loans and credit cards.
  • Indus Classic Salary Account: Basic zero balance account with standard debit card and transaction limits.

Eligibility

Salaried employees through employer tie-ups with IndusInd Bank are eligible.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Rental Agreement)
  • Employment Proof (Salary Slip or Employment Letter)
  • Recent Passport-sized Photographs
