AU Small Finance Bank Personal Loan

1. Loan Amount and Eligibility

  • Loan Amount: AU Small Finance Bank offers loans ranging from ₹50,000 to ₹25 lakh, depending on the borrower’s profile, credit score, and repayment capacity.
  • Salaried Individuals: Age between 21 to 58 years with a stable income source.
  • Self-Employed: Must have a stable income source and a good credit history, aged between 21 to 65 years.

2. Interest Rate

  • Interest Rates: AU Small Finance Bank offers competitive interest rates ranging from 12% to 24% per annum, depending on the applicant’s credit profile, loan tenure, and loan amount.

3. Loan Tenure

  • Flexible Repayment Terms: Loan tenure can range from 12 months to 60 months, allowing borrowers to choose a plan based on their financial situation.

4. Documentation Required

  • KYC Documents: Aadhaar card, PAN card, Passport, or Voter ID for identity and address proof.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Salary slips for the last 3 months, bank statements for the last 6 months, Form 16, or ITR.
    • Self-Employed: Business financials, ITRs, bank statements, and other income-related documents.
  • Photograph: A recent passport-size photograph.

5. Processing Fees and Charges

  • Processing Fee: Typically 2% to 4% of the loan amount.
  • Prepayment Charges: Generally 2% to 4% of the outstanding principal, depending on the conditions.
  • Late Payment Fees: Around 2% of the overdue amount per month.
  • Other Charges: Includes charges for loan cancellation, statement requests, and legal/technical verification fees.

6. Approval Time and Disbursement

  • Quick Processing: AU Small Finance Bank offers rapid loan approval and disbursement, with eligible loans processed within 48 hours.
  • Disbursement: Once approved, the loan amount is credited directly to the borrower’s bank account.

7. Application Process

  • Online Application: Customers can apply through AU Small Finance Bank’s website or mobile app by submitting the required details and documents.
  • Offline Application: Applicants can also visit any AU Small Finance Bank branch for assistance in the application process.

8. Features and Benefits

  • No Collateral Required: AU Small Finance Bank personal loans are unsecured, requiring no collateral or guarantor.
  • Flexible Repayment Options: Borrowers can choose a repayment plan suited to their income and lifestyle.
  • Balance Transfer: AU Small Finance Bank allows balance transfers of existing personal loans from other banks at competitive interest rates.
  • Top-Up Facility: Top-up loans are available to eligible borrowers based on their repayment history and requirements.
