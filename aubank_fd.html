AU Small Finance Bank Fixed Deposits

AU Small Finance Bank offers a range of Fixed Deposit (FD) options to help you achieve your financial goals with security and attractive returns. Below are the details of the available FD products.

Types of Fixed Deposits

  • Regular Fixed Deposit: For general investment with flexible tenure.
  • Tax Saving Fixed Deposit: Provides tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
  • Senior Citizens Fixed Deposit: Special FD with higher interest rates for senior citizens.

Features

Feature Description
Interest Rates Varies based on the tenure and type of deposit.
Tenure Ranges from 7 days to 10 years.
Minimum Deposit Amount As low as Rs. 1,000.
Premature Withdrawal Allowed with penalties.
Loan Against FD Available.
Automatic Renewal Option available.

Benefits

  • Flexible Tenure: Choose from various tenures to suit your financial goals.
  • Interest Payouts: Options for monthly, quarterly, or annual payouts.
  • Security: Safe and secure investment with guaranteed returns.
  • Tax Benefits: Available for certain types of deposits (Tax Saving FD).
