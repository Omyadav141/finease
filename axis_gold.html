Axis Bank Gold Loan

1. Loan Amount

  • Minimum Loan Amount: Generally starts from as low as ₹25,001.
  • Maximum Loan Amount: Based on the gold’s value, it can go up to ₹20 lakh, but eligibility may vary based on the weight and purity of the gold provided as collateral.

2. Eligibility

  • Age: Applicants typically need to be between 18 and 75 years old.
  • Occupation: Open to salaried, self-employed, businesspersons, and farmers.
  • Gold Requirements: The gold should be in the form of jewelry or ornaments with purity between 18 and 22 carats.

3. Interest Rates and Fees

  • Interest Rate: Rates usually start around 9% to 15% per annum, depending on the loan amount and tenure.
  • Processing Fees: Approximately 1% of the loan amount, plus applicable GST.
  • Prepayment Charges: Generally no charges for prepayment, but this may vary based on loan conditions.

4. Loan Tenure

  • Tenure Options: Flexible tenure options range from 6 months to 36 months, allowing borrowers to choose based on their repayment capability.

5. Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio

  • Axis Bank typically follows the RBI-mandated LTV of up to 75% of the gold’s market value.
  • This means customers can avail a loan of up to 75% of their gold's appraised value.

6. Disbursement and Documentation

  • Quick Disbursement: Loans are typically disbursed within a few hours if documents are in place.
  • Documentation Required:
    • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Voter ID, or Driver’s License)
    • Address Proof (Utility Bill, Aadhaar, or other official documents)
    • Recently taken photographs of the applicant

7. Repayment Options

  • Regular EMI: Monthly installments.
  • Bullet Repayment: Entire principal and interest are paid at the end of the tenure.
  • Overdraft Facility: Interest is only charged on the utilized amount, providing flexibility.

8. Features and Benefits

  • No Credit Score Requirement: Since it’s a secured loan, there’s no stringent credit score check.
  • Minimal Documentation: Quick processing with minimal paperwork.
  • Multiple Repayment Options: Flexibility in choosing a repayment method based on customer needs.
  • Insurance Cover: The gold pledged is generally insured against theft or damage, providing additional security to the borrower.

9. Application Process

  • Offline: Applicants can visit any Axis Bank branch with the required documents and gold.
  • Online Application: Some branches and online platforms allow applicants to pre-apply, but final processing must be completed in person.
