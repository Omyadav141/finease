IndusInd Bank - Fixed Deposit Account

Features of IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposit

  • Interest Rates: Competitive interest rates that vary depending on the deposit amount and tenure. Higher interest rates for senior citizens.
  • Flexible Tenure: Choose a deposit tenure ranging from 7 days to 10 years.
  • Premature Withdrawal: Option for premature withdrawal with applicable penalties.
  • Loan Against FD: You can avail of a loan against your FD, typically up to 90% of the FD amount.
  • Automatic Renewal: The FD can be renewed automatically upon maturity, based on the account holder's instructions.
  • Nomination Facility: You can nominate someone to receive the FD amount in the event of your demise.
  • Tax-Saving FD: A special scheme under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act, offering tax benefits.

Types of IndusInd Bank Fixed Deposit Accounts

  • Regular Fixed Deposit: Minimum Deposit starts from ₹10,000, Tenure from 7 days to 10 years, interest payout options include cumulative or non-cumulative.
  • Senior Citizen Fixed Deposit: Higher interest rates for citizens aged 60 and above, with flexible payout options.
  • Tax Saver Fixed Deposit: Enjoy tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act. Minimum deposit ₹100, lock-in period of 5 years.
  • Flexi Fixed Deposit: Linked to your savings account for automatic sweep-in and sweep-out, providing liquidity while earning FD interest rates.

Fixed Deposit Interest Rates

Customer Type Interest Rate

Senior Citizens 0.50% more than the regular rates

Premature Withdrawal

IndusInd Bank allows premature withdrawal of fixed deposits with applicable penalties, which depend on the tenure of the deposit and the remaining period at the time of withdrawal. Tax-Saving FDs cannot be withdrawn prematurely during the 5-year lock-in period.

Loan Against Fixed Deposit

  • Loan Amount: Up to 90% of the FD amount.
  • Interest Rate: Typically 1% to 2% higher than the FD interest rate.
  • Loan Tenure: Linked to the FD tenure, not exceeding the FD term.
  • Benefit: Retain FD interest earnings while accessing liquidity.

Fixed Deposit for NRI Customers

  • NRE Fixed Deposit: Fully repatriable, tax-free interest, tenure from 1 to 10 years.
  • NRO Fixed Deposit: Non-repatriable principal, but interest is repatriable, tenure from 7 days to 10 years.
  • FCNR Fixed Deposit: Available in multiple currencies, tenure from 1 to 5 years, tax-free interest.

Renewal and Auto-Renewal

Upon maturity, the FD can be automatically renewed based on the account holder’s instructions or manually renewed via branch or internet banking.

How to Open a Fixed Deposit Account

You can open an FD account online through the IndusNet internet banking portal, the IndusMobile app, or by visiting a branch. Relationship managers are available for premium customers.
