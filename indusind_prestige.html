Eligibility Criteria

Available for individuals aged 18+ with higher income or investment portfolios.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar card, PAN card, Passport, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (Utility Bill, Rental Agreement, etc.)
  • Passport-sized photographs

Minimum Balance Requirement

Urban/Metro: ₹2 lakhs. Semi-Urban/Rural: Slightly lower requirements may apply.

Fees and Charges

  • No non-maintenance charges if account activity meets minimum requirements.
  • Free cheque book issuance and debit card.

Exclusive Lifestyle Benefits

Special offers across dining, travel, and shopping. Access to 24/7 concierge services.
