AU Small Finance Bank Car Loan

1. Purpose and Key Features

  • Loan Purpose: Financing for the purchase of new and used cars.
  • Loan Amount: Covers up to 100% of the car's on-road price for new vehicles and up to 90% of the valuation for used vehicles.
  • Flexible Tenure: Ranges from 1 to 7 years, based on the repayment capacity.
  • Interest Rates: Competitive rates, dependent on applicant profile, loan tenure, and car model.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21-60 years for salaried and self-employed individuals.
  • Employment/Income:
    • Salaried: Minimum 1-year employment stability with a steady income.
    • Self-employed: At least 2 years in business with a stable income.
  • Credit Score: A good credit score improves chances of approval and favorable interest rates.

3. Documents Required

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar, PAN card, Passport, or Voter ID.
  • Address Proof: Utility bill, rental agreement, or Passport.
  • Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Latest salary slips, bank statements, and Form 16.
    • Self-employed: ITR, bank statements, business proof.
  • Vehicle Documents: Car quotation, registration certificate, insurance papers, and valuation report (for used cars).

4. Fees and Charges

  • Processing Fee: Around 1-2% of the loan amount, but may vary.
  • Prepayment Charges: Around 2-5% of the outstanding amount for early repayment.
  • Late Payment Penalty: Charged as per bank policy, typically a percentage of the overdue EMI.

5. Special Features

  • Quick Processing: Fast disbursal, especially for pre-approved customers.
  • Top-up Loan Facility: Additional loan for eligible existing car loan customers.
  • Insurance and Add-Ons: Options for car insurance and additional protection plans.

6. Application Process

  • Online Application: Apply through the AU Small Finance Bank website or app by filling out the online form.
  • Branch Visit: Visit the nearest branch with the necessary documents.
