Kotak Mahindra Gold Loan

1. Loan Features

  • Loan Amount: Loans range from ₹20,000 to ₹50 lakh, based on the value and quality of the gold pledged.
  • Loan Tenure: Available for 6 months to 36 months, offering flexibility based on borrower needs.
  • Interest Rates: Competitive rates, usually starting from 7-10% per annum, depending on loan tenure and amount.
  • Gold Valuation: Loan amount is determined by the purity (karats) and weight of the gold, typically accepting gold jewelry.
  • Repayment Options: Flexible options including EMI, bullet repayment (interest monthly, principal at tenure end), and partial payments.

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: The borrower must be at least 18 years old and capable of providing self-attested ID proof.
  • Income Criteria: No minimum income requirement, making gold loans accessible to various income groups.
  • Loan-to-Value Ratio (LTV): Borrowers can receive up to 75% of the gold's assessed market value as per RBI guidelines.

3. Required Documents

  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License.
  • Address Proof: Recent Utility Bills, Passport, or Aadhaar with the correct address.
  • Photographs: Passport-sized photographs of the applicant.

4. Processing Time and Charges

  • Quick Processing: Kotak gold loans are processed quickly, often within hours if documentation is complete.
  • Processing Fees: Typically ranges from 0.25% to 1% of the loan amount, depending on the loan amount and tenure.
  • Additional Fees: May include foreclosure charges, late payment fees, and gold valuation charges. Foreclosure charges range from 2-4% if the loan is closed early.

5. Advantages of Kotak Gold Loan

  • Instant Funds: Receive quick funds due to the simple and fast processing of the loan.
  • Flexible Repayment Plans: Tailored repayment options for convenient servicing of the loan.
  • No Income Proof Required: Only the gold needs to be pledged, no credit history verification required.
  • Safe Custody of Gold: Kotak offers secure storage for pledged gold.

6. Application Process

  • In-Branch Application: Visit a Kotak Bank branch with the necessary gold and documents for assessment.
  • Online Application: Start the loan application online, followed by a Kotak representative's follow-up for assessment and documentation.
