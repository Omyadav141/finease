IDFC FIRST Bank - Salary Account

Introducing the IDFC FIRST Bank Salary Account, designed to offer a range of benefits and features tailored for salaried individuals. This account provides a seamless banking experience with added convenience and exclusive features.

Features

  • No Minimum Balance Requirement: Enjoy the flexibility of a zero-balance account with no monthly maintenance charges.
  • Free Debit Card: Receive a complimentary debit card with no annual fee.
  • Online and Mobile Banking: Access your account anytime, anywhere with our easy-to-use digital platforms.
  • Priority Banking Services: Avail personalized services and assistance through dedicated relationship managers.
  • Exclusive Offers: Benefit from special offers on loans, insurance, and more.

Eligibility

  • Indian citizens
  • Individuals with a regular salary income
  • Age 21 years and above
