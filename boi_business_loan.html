Bank of India (BOI) Business Loans

1. Loan Types

  • Term Loan for Business Expansion
  • Working Capital Finance
  • SME Loans (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises)
  • Loan Against Property (LAP)
  • Commercial Vehicle Loan
  • Export Finance
  • Project Finance

2. Loan Amount

  • Term Loan for Business Expansion: ₹5 lakh to ₹25 crore or more.
  • Working Capital Finance: Up to ₹5 crore.
  • SME Loans: ₹50,000 to ₹5 crore.
  • Loan Against Property (LAP): Up to 70% of the property’s market value.
  • Commercial Vehicle Loan: Up to 90% of the vehicle cost.
  • Export Finance: Based on export value and customer order size.

3. Interest Rates

  • General rates: 10% to 14% p.a., depending on the loan type and profile.
  • SME Loans: Lower rates for a good credit track record.
  • LAP: 9% to 12% p.a.

4. Repayment Tenure

  • Working Capital Finance: Short-term (up to 12 months, renewable).
  • Term Loan for Business Expansion: Up to 10 years.
  • SME Loans: Usually 5 to 7 years.
  • LAP: Up to 15 years.
  • Commercial Vehicle Loan: Typically 5 to 7 years.

5. Features

  • SME Loans with flexible repayment terms and subsidized rates under government schemes.
  • Working Capital Loans for day-to-day business expenses like wages and raw materials.
  • Loan Against Property for secured loans using residential, commercial, or industrial properties.
  • Commercial Vehicle Loans for trucks, buses, and other vehicles.
  • Export Finance to support international trade and manage export-related capital needs.
  • Access to Mudra and MSME schemes with subsidies.

6. Eligibility

  • Businesses, entrepreneurs, and corporates.
  • Minimum age: 21 years; Maximum age at loan maturity: 65 years.
  • Good credit history (typically a credit score of 650 or higher).
  • Stable business operations for at least 2–3 years.
  • For LAP, ownership of property is mandatory.

7. Documents Required

  • Business registration proof.
  • Financial statements (balance sheet, profit & loss account).
  • Proof of ownership (for LAP and commercial vehicle loans).
  • Bank statements and income tax returns for the past 2 years.
  • KYC documents (Aadhaar, PAN, address proof).
  • Proof of collateral (for secured loans like LAP).
