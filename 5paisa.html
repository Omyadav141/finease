Company Overview

5paisa is a prominent discount brokerage firm in India, known for providing affordable financial services to retail investors. It operates entirely online and offers a wide range of financial products and services, from stock trading to investment in mutual funds and insurance.

  • Founded: 2016
  • Parent Company: IIFL (India Infoline)
  • Headquarters: Mumbai, India
  • Core Business: Discount broking, low-cost services

Products and Services

  • Demat and Trading Accounts: For stocks, commodities, currencies, and ETFs
  • Mutual Funds: Direct mutual fund investments with no commission
  • Initial Public Offerings (IPOs): Access to invest in IPOs directly
  • Robo Advisory: AI-based personalized portfolio recommendations
  • Insurance: Health, life, and term insurance products
  • Loans: Personal loans through partner companies

Brokerage Plan

  • Basic Plan: Flat ₹20 per order across all segments
  • Power Investor Plan: ₹499/month with lower brokerage rates
  • Ultra Trader Plan: ₹999/month for lowest brokerage rates

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Low-cost brokerage fees
  • Wide range of financial products
  • User-friendly platforms
  • Robo Advisory and detailed research reports

Cons

  • No physical branches
  • Limited customer support options
  • Fewer advanced trading features

Charges and Fees

  • Brokerage: ₹20 per trade (Basic Plan)
  • AMC: ₹300-₹500/year (Demat account)
  • Other Fees: Nominal fees for fund transfers and transactions
