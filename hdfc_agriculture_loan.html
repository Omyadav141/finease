HDFC Bank Agriculture Loan Options

1. Retail Agri Loan (Kisan Credit Card & Kisan Gold Card)

  • Loan Amount: Determined based on factors like land under cultivation, cropping pattern, and scale of finance.
  • Interest Rates: Starting from 9% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: Flexible repayment tenure up to 5 years.
  • Features:
    • Cash Credit/Overdraft facilities for agricultural expenses like crop production and maintenance of farm assets.
    • Term Loans for investment purposes such as land development and purchasing farm implements.
    • Triple-layered security for gold pledged as collateral.
    • Preferential rates for women, agricultural/priority sector, and existing HDFC Bank customers.
    • Free personal accident insurance coverage of ₹2 lakhs and crop insurance under Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana.
    • Rupay Farmer Platinum Debit Card for easy transactions.
  • Eligibility: Individual farmers or joint borrowers, with applicants up to 60 years of age. For applicants above 60 years, a legal heir is required as a co-applicant.

2. Agri Gold Loan

  • Loan Amount: Ranges from ₹25,000 to ₹25 lakhs.
  • Interest Rates: Starting from 9% per annum.
  • Repayment Tenure: Flexible tenure ranging from 12 to 42 months.
  • Features:
    • Quick processing for prompt fund disbursement.
    • Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio up to 75% of the gold's value pledged.
    • Professional gold valuation ensures a fair assessment of the gold's value.
    • Competitive interest rates designed for farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs.
    • Funds can be used for crop production, farm equipment, irrigation systems, etc.
  • Eligibility: Individuals engaged in agriculture/agri-allied activities with gold ornaments, jewelry, or specially minted gold coins (up to 50 grams per borrower).

General Features of HDFC Bank Agriculture Loans

  • No income proof needed, simplifying the application process.
  • Option for part payment before the loan tenure ends.
  • No foreclosure charges after three months.
  • High Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio up to 75% for gold loans.
  • Insurance options including personal accident cover and crop insurance.
  • 24/7 customer service for all queries and concerns.
  • Online access for managing loan status, repayment schedules, and transaction history.

Additional Benefits of HDFC Bank Agriculture Loans

  • Loans designed to meet the specific needs of farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs.
  • Support for various agricultural activities including crop production, equipment purchases, and capital investments.
  • Easy access through HDFC Bank’s extensive branch network and digital banking platforms.
  • Government schemes and subsidies, including Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana.
