Bank of India (BOI) Education Loans

1. Loan Amount

  • For studies in India: Up to ₹20 lakh.
  • For studies abroad: Up to ₹30 lakh.

2. Interest Rates

  • Starting from 8.25% to 9.50% p.a., depending on the loan amount and applicant's profile.

3. Repayment Tenure

  • Up to 15 years, with a moratorium period (repayment begins after course completion).

4. Features

  • No collateral required for loans up to ₹4 lakh.
  • Loan covers tuition fees, hostel fees, books, exam fees, travel, etc.
  • Tax benefits available under Section 80E for interest payments.
  • Option for top-up loans for further studies.
  • Quick disbursal and processing.
  • Processing fee: Typically 1% of the loan amount.
  • No prepayment charges after the moratorium period.

5. Eligibility

  • Indian students who have secured admission to a recognized institution (India or abroad).
  • Age: Typically between 18 to 35 years.
  • Good academic record is required.
  • Co-applicant required (usually parents or guardians).

6. Documents Required

  • Proof of identity: Aadhaar, Passport, PAN, etc.
  • Admission letter from the educational institution.
  • Proof of academic qualifications: Mark sheets, certificates.
  • Income proof of co-applicant: Salary slips, ITR, bank statements.
  • Passport-sized photographs.
